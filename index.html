I'm J. Matthew Griffis, usually called 'Matt,' and I do creative coding and interaction design.

I specialize in creating 'lasagna' experiences, i.e. rich, multi-layered, and full of sauce, with a generous yet curated helping of cheese.

Whatever the format, from websites to critical software to games, my work encourages exploration and play.

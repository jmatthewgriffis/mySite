Experiments in Unity3D: a series of vignettes

  1. "Pep talk"--the Emperor addresses the Stormtroopers before a big battle [not interactive].
  2. "It's a trap!"--preparing for the Jedi's approach from the plains, a Stormtrooper has constructed an elaborate trap in the mountains [not interactive].
  3. "Face-off"--a Stormtrooper and a Jedi meet in battle. The Stormtrooper comes off worse [not interactive].
  4. "A rare win"--a Stormtrooper scores a victory with the quick use of a blaster pistol [click the suspension cable].

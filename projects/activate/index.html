Lights Off

PDF Resource Files

[Original description] "In this game, your neighbors have become used to leaving their lights on all day long, so it is up to you to help them change their behavior and learn how to use energy more efficiently. In order to do this, you must spread the word through meeting houses your neighbors visit. But be careful! As you walk around the neighborhood you must avoid the neighbors who are still using too much energy. Their bad habits have transformed them into monsters who cannot control their desire to keep using energy. Can you save your neighbors by convincing them to act more responsibly and turn off all the light"

[Controls] Use the Arrow keys to move your character. Touch the gray meeting houses to spread the word about turning lights off. Your neighbors are monstrous and must be avoided, but if they bump into a house with lights off, they'll come to their senses and help spread the word!
